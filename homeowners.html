Home Renovations

Diversified Holdings NW LLC is your trusted partner for all your construction, remodeling, and renovation needs. With a focus on both enhancing the aesthetics of your home and catering to your personal preferences, we offer a wide range of services to transform your house into the dream home you've always envisioned.

For homeowners looking to elevate their curb appeal, we specialize in driveway and walkway renovations, ensuring a welcoming entrance to your property. Our expert craftsmen are skilled in deck building, creating outdoor spaces for relaxation and entertainment. We also excel in designing and constructing outdoor covered patios, allowing you to enjoy the outdoors while staying protected from the elements.

Inside your home, we offer a comprehensive suite of services, including painting, to give your interiors a fresh, vibrant look. Our interior renovations encompass everything from updating living spaces to customizing bathrooms and kitchens. We're experts in bath and kitchen tile work, installing cabinets that optimize storage and functionality, and laying down flooring that not only looks stunning but also stands the test of time.

With Diversified Holdings NW LLC, you can trust in our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and dedication to bringing your vision to life, whether it's for personal enjoyment or to increase your home's market appeal. We're your one-stop destination for turning your house into the perfect home, tailored to your desires and needs.
