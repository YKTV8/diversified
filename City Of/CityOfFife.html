City of Fife

This project aims to extend crucial utilities, enhance security, improve lighting, optimize land use, and establish the foundations and utilities necessary for buildings in the City of Fife Public Works laydown yard.


  1. Extension of Sewer Service, Water Service, and Electrical: This project includes extending sewer service, water service, and electrical connections. This means that infrastructure will be put in place to allow for the transport of sewage, the delivery of fresh water, and the supply of electrical power from the City of Fife Public Works driveway to the City of Fife Public Works laydown yard. This extension is crucial for supporting various operations and facilities at the laydown yard.
  2. Chain Link Fence Installation (395 feet): A chain link fence extending for 395 feet will be installed as part of the project. Chain link fences are commonly used for security and boundary purposes. In this context, it may serve to demarcate the laydown yard's boundaries, enhance security, or provide safety around specific areas.
  3. Driveway Luminaire: A driveway luminaire refers to a lighting fixture installed near or along a driveway or entrance. This lighting is essential for visibility and safety, especially during nighttime or low-light conditions. The luminaire will ensure that the driveway area is well-lit, promoting safety for vehicles and pedestrians using this access point.
  4. Site Grading:Site grading involves the modification of the land's contours and elevations to achieve specific design goals. In this project, site grading may be necessary to ensure proper drainage, level surfaces, or other site-specific requirements. Proper grading is crucial for the efficient and safe use of the laydown yard.
  5. Concrete Building Foundations:The construction project includes the installation of concrete building foundations. Building foundations are critical structural components that support the weight of the buildings or structures above them. The foundations need to be strong and stable to ensure the safety and longevity of the structures.
  6. Building Utilities Connections:This aspect of the project involves connecting the various utilities required for the buildings in the laydown yard. This can include water supply lines, electrical connections, sewage systems, and other utilities necessary for the functioning of the buildings. Proper utility connections are essential for the operational efficiency of the facilities.

Overview

Overall, this construction project aims to enhance the functionality and infrastructure of the City of Fife Public Works laydown yard. It involves extending critical services, installing security measures, ensuring proper lighting, modifying the site for optimal use, and establishing a solid foundation for buildings while connecting essential utilities. These improvements will likely contribute to the efficiency, safety, and overall effectiveness of the City of Fife's public works operations in this area.
