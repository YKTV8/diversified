The major items of work include preparing the existing surface for storm water improvements and imported soils, importing and compacting said soils, paving of the pump track, and final site stabilization.

  • Preparation of Existing Surface:Clear and grade the current surface to ensure it is suitable for stormwater improvements and the application of imported soils.
  • Importing Soils:Bring in the required soils from external sources to meet project specifications.
  • Compacting Imported Soils:Compact the imported soils to achieve the desired density and stability for construction.
  • Stormwater Improvements:Implement necessary measures to manage stormwater, which may include installing drainage systems, retention basins, or other infrastructure.
  • Paving of the Pump Track:Construct and pave the pump track, ensuring a smooth and durable surface suitable for its intended use.
  • Final Site Stabilization:Perform finishing work to stabilize the site, including landscaping, erosion control, and any other necessary measures to ensure the site remains in good condition.
