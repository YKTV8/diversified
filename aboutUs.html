Meet Our Professionals

At Diversified Holdings NW, we are more than just a construction company; we are a family-operated business built on a foundation of passion, dedication, and over 35 years of diverse expertise in the construction industry. Our journey began with a commitment to quality, integrity, and a vision to transform dreams into reality. Today, we proudly stand as a trusted name in both public works and residential remodeling, leaving an indelible mark on every project we undertake.


Matt Webb

With 22 years of dedicated experience, Matt Webb stands as a seasoned business owner and industry visionary in the Field Services Operations (FSO) sector. His expertise spans critical domains, including wireless department development, contract negotiations, talent acquisition, and adept project management. Here's a brief glimpse into Matt's remarkable journey:

Matt Webb's visionary leadership has propelled the wireless department within his organization to unprecedented heights. His strategic prowess and resource allocation acumen have translated into substantial revenue streams, shaping the wireless landscape with foresight and innovation.

In the world of contract negotiations, Matt is a true virtuoso. Whether navigating a $100,000 deal or orchestrating multimillion-dollar agreements worth $15 million, his approach is unwaveringly effective. He's known for securing favorable terms that maximize value and foster mutually beneficial partnerships.

Matt's passion for cultivating excellence extends to talent recruitment and team building. He understands that a thriving organization hinges on the caliber of its professionals. Matt's talent-spotting expertise has been pivotal in driving his company's growth and success. Critical to his role is Matt's meticulous review and approval of contract bids, ensuring they align with strategic objectives and financial targets. His swift decision-making has consistently secured projects valued up to $5 million, cementing his company's competitive edge. Matt thrives under pressure and excels in creating corrective action plans that steer challenging projects toward success. His ability to think critically, adapt nimbly, and inspire his teams has delivered consistently outstanding results.

Matt Webb's 22-year journey epitomizes excellence in FSO. He combines deep industry knowledge with a knack for innovation to drive his business forward. As he continues to lead and inspire his team, Matt remains dedicated to pioneering innovative solutions that keep his company at the forefront of the FSO industry.
